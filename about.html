Saothar Ealaíne was the villa of the famous art collector Jugs McBulge until 1946. After his death, to honour him, the local authorities decided to turn the place into an art gallery. Since then, the gallery has been conducting exhibitions every week and is an avenue for local artists and others to display their works.
