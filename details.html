Irish Expression

Exhibition Irish Expression is an exhibition that showcases the most famous works of three prominent Irish painters: Francis Bacon, John Luke and Roderic O’Connor. The site includes some of the paintings that will be displayed at Saothar Ealaíne. The exhibition is an opportunity for enthusiasts and art afficionados to learn more about the works of the artists.
